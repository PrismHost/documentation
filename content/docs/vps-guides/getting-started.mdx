## Steps

<Steps>
    <Step>
        #### Check Your Email  
        After purchasing a VPS, you'll receive an email with your **VPS panel login details**. 
        
        This includes:
        - Panel URL  
        - Username (usually your email)  
        - Password
        
        > If you haven't purchased one, you can check out our [Premium](https://prism-host.com/pvps) & [Budget](https://prism-host.com/bvps) VPS Plans!

        > **Note**: We **highly** recommend you reset this password for security. [Here's a guide](/vps-guides/reset-password) on how you can reset it
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Log in to the VPS Panel  
        Visit the link provided in your email and enter your credentials to access your VPS dashboard.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Select and Manage Your VPS  
        On the VPS panel dashboard, you'll see a list of your active VPS instances.  
        Click **Manage** next to the VPS you want to work with.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Install Your Operating System  
        Once on the VPS management page, you can install your preferred OS using the built-in installer.

        > Follow our full guide here:  
        [How to Install an Operating System](/vps-guides/installing-an-os)
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Connect to Your VPS  
        Once your OS is installed and ready, you can connect to your server:

        - **Windows VPS** - Connect using Remote Desktop Connection  
        - **Linux VPS** - Connect using SSH via Windows Terminal  

        > View the full connection guide here:  
        [How to Connect to Your VPS](/vps-guides/connecting)
    </Step>
</Steps>
