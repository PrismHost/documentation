## Activating Your Affiliate Account

If you'd like to earn recurring commissions by referring customers to us, you can easily activate your affiliate account through the client portal.

### Steps

<Steps>
    <Step>
        #### Log in to the Client Portal  
        Head over to [client.prism-host.com](https://client.prism-host.com) and log in with your account.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Go to the Affiliates Page  
        On the top navigation bar, click on **Affiliates** or visit [client.prism-host.com/affiliates.php](https://client.prism-host.com/affiliates.php).
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Activate Your Affiliate Account  
        Click the green **Activate Affiliate Account** button on the page.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### You're now an affiliate!  
        Once activated, you'll be able to:
        - View your referral link
        - Track clicks, sign-ups, and conversions
        - Monitor your commission balance
        - Request withdrawals when eligible

        Simply share your referral link - as long as someone signs up using your link and places an order, you'll earn recurring commission on their active services.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Deactivating Your Affiliate Account

If you no longer wish to participate in the affiliate program, you can request deactivation at any time.

### Steps

<Steps>
    <Step>
        #### Submit a Support Request  
        To deactivate your affiliate account, please [contact us](https://prism-host.com/contact).
        > **Note:** Deactivating your affiliate account will **permanently** delete all affiliate data - including your referral history and any unpaid commissions. This action is **irreversible**.
    </Step>
</Steps>
