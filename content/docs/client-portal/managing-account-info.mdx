## Steps

<Steps>
    <Step>
        #### Log in to the Client Portal  
        Head over to [client.prism-host.com](https://client.prism-host.com) and log in with your account credentials.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Access Account Settings  
        Click your name in the top-right corner of the portal. From the dropdown, choose the section you wish to manage:
        - **[Account Details](https://client.prism-host.com/clientarea.php?action=details)** – update contact info like name, address, and phone number  
        - **[Payment Methods](https://client.prism-host.com/account/paymentmethods)** – manage saved cards or other payment methods  
        - **[Your Profile](https://client.prism-host.com/user/profile)** – update login email, password, and other profile settings
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Make Your Changes  
        On the selected page, edit the information you'd like to change.
        > Some sections may require you to scroll down and click **Save Changes** at the bottom to apply updates.
    </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Important Notes

- **Payment Method Requirement**  
  If you've ever had an active service on your account, you must have **at least one valid payment method** on file at all times.  
  If you need your only saved payment method removed, please [contact us](https://prism-host.com/contact) and we'll assist you.

If you have any questions or run into issues while updating your information, don't hesitate to [reach out to us](https://prism-host.com/contact).
