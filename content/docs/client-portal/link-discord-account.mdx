## Linking Your Account

Follow the steps below to connect your Discord account to your client portal account.

### Steps

<Steps>
    <Step>
        #### Log in to the Client Portal  
        Go to [client.prism-host.com](https://client.prism-host.com) and log in with your credentials.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Locate the "Discord Link" Card  
        On the client portal homepage, find the card labeled **Discord Link**.  
        <img src={DiscordLink.src} />
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Click "Link Discord"  
        Click the blue **Link Discord** button at the top right of the card.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Authorize the Link  
        A Discord window will open - click **Authorize** to link your Discord account.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Done!  
        You'll be redirected back to the client portal and see a confirmation message.

        > **Note:** It may take up to **30 minutes** for roles to be applied in Discord.  
        If your roles haven't appeared after that time, please [open a ticket on our Discord](https://prism-host.com/discord) and weâ€™ll be happy to assist you.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Unlinking Your Account

Need to disconnect your Discord account? Follow the steps below to unlink it from your client portal account.

### Steps

<Steps>
    <Step>
        #### Log in to the Client Portal  
        Head to [client.prism-host.com](https://client.prism-host.com) and sign in.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Locate the "Discord Link" Card  
        On the client portal homepage, find the card labeled **Discord Link**.  
        <img src={DiscordUnlink.src} />
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Click "Unlink Discord"  
        Click the blue **Unlink Discord** button at the top right of the card.
    </Step>
    <Step>
        #### Success!  
        You'll be redirected back to the client portal and see a success message confirming your account has been unlinked.
    </Step>
</Steps>
