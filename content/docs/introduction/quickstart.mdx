## Getting Started

Welcome! Follow the instructions below to learn how to rent a server to get started on your next project!

### 1. Choose A Service

First, go to our [website](https://prism-host.com) and either click the "View Plans" button or use the navigation bar at the top to choose the best service to fit your needs.

<Callout title="Need help choosing?" type="info">
    [Reach out to us](https://prism-host.com/contact) and we are more than happy to recommend a service based on what you need.
</Callout>
### 2. Checkout

Once you've chosen the service you want, click on "CONFIGURE" or "ORDER NOW" on the plan you want, this will either prompt you to configure your plan or bring you to the checkout page.

When you've configured the plan to your liking, continue to the next step to fill out your account information & payment method. We accept Credit & Debit cards through Stripe, if you need a different payment method, please [reach out](https://prism-host.com/contact) and we might be able to make an accommodation.

### 3. Setting up your service

Once you've paid for your service, you will receive an email containing the information you need to set it up. 

- For Eco & Premium VPS, you will need to login to the VPS management Panel to being using your VPS. Click [here](/vps-guides/getting-started) to learn how to get started with your new VPS
- For game servers & Discord bots, you will need to login to our game server management panel, called "ARC" and begin setting up your new server. Click [here](/game-guides/getting-started) to learn how to get started with your new game server.
- For Dedicated Servers, you will receive an email containing login information to our dedicated server management panel, if you chose an operating system during checkout you will receive an email containing the SSH/RDP information for your new server. Click [here](/dedicated-server-guides/getting-started) for more information.
- For website hosting, you will receive an email containing login information to our website management panel, called cPanel. From there you will be able to manage your website.

### 4. Connect to your server
After configuring everything necessary, you can startup your server and connect to it. This process varies per service so we have dedicated guides on how to connect to your server.

- [Connecting to your VPS](/vps-guides/connecting)
- [Connecting to your game server](/game-guides/connecting)
- [Connecting to your dedicated server](/dedicated-server-guides/connecting)

### All done! 

And just like that, your service is setup and you can start using it. You can further configure the server by customizing settings or installing mods/plugs if they are available. 

Need help? Feel free to [contact us](https://prism-host.com/contact) and our helpful support team will be with you ASAP.
