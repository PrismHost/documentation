Welcome to Prism Host! We're excited to offer **24-hour free trials** so you can explore our services before committing to a paid plan.

<Callout title="Note:" type="info">
  Free trials are **subject to availability** and are limited to **one per service per customer**. Abuse of the free trial system will result in account termination.
</Callout>

---

## How to Start Your Free Trial

1. Click on the **"Try for free"** button on our [home page](https://prism-host.com).
2. Choose a service from the popup menu.
    - Looking for a service not listed? [Contact our support team](https://prism-host.com/contact) to request access.
3. Select your **preferred location**, then click **"Continue"**.
4. Click **"Checkout"**.
    - If you see an amount due today other than **0.00**, your account has already used the free trial for that service and it is no longer available to you.
5. Your trial server will usually deploy **automatically**.
    - If not, contact our support team and we'll get you set up.  
    > Support time **does not** count against your 24-hour trial.

<Callout title="Don't Lose Your Data" type="warn">
  Trial servers are **automatically deactivated after 24 hours**. Be sure to Back up your data before the trial ends or upgrade to a paid plan to retain your service
</Callout>


## How to Upgrade Your Service

1. Log in to the [client portal](https://client.prism-host.com).
2. Click on **"Services"** in the menu and choose **"My Services"**.
3. Select the trial service you want to upgrade and click **"Manage"**.
4. On the left sidebar, click the **"Upgrade"** button.
